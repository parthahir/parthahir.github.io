How can we stop these irreversible effects

Here are a few strategies:

  • Be resource friendly. Meaning, only drive when needed, conserve water as much as possible (while brushing your teeth), be mindful of the lights around you and turn them off if they are not being used.
  • Spread the word!
  • Believe in the power of numbers
  • Go vegetarian
Climate change stems from many dynamic factors, one of which includes our food system. We need a more sustainable agrifood system:
  • An economically, socially, environmentally sustainable agrifood system includes a localized food system that incorporates promotes the increase and accessibility of farmer markets that would allow individuals to support local farmers
  • A sustainable agrifood system would have a sector that would provide the means to support lcal companies and restaurants to boost the nearby economy.
  • To achieve sustainablity, more farmers markets must be accessed by the greater population
